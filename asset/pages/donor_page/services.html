Our Services

Explore the various ways you can contribute and the support services available for our valued donors.

Whole Blood Donation

The most common type of donation where you give about a pint of whole blood.

  • Saves up to 3 lives
  • Takes about 1 hour
  • Essential for trauma & surgery

Platelet Donation

Platelets are tiny cell fragments that help stop bleeding by helping blood to clot.

  • Vital for cancer patients
  • Donated every 7 days
  • Takes about 2-3 hours

Plasma Donation

Plasma is the liquid portion of blood used to treat rare chronic conditions.

  • AB Elite Plasma
  • Used for burn victims
  • Frequent donations possible

Comprehensive Health Screening

Every donation includes a mini-physical and health check. We monitor your hemoglobin, pulse, blood pressure, and more.

  • Hemoglobin Test
  • BP Monitoring
  • Pulse Check
  • Blood Type ID

Emergency Response Team

Join our priority contact list for urgent blood shortages. Be the first to know when your specific blood type is critical for a life-saving situation.
