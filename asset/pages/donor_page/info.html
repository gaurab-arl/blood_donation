Learn About Blood Donation

Everything you need to know about eligibility, donation process, and benefits.

Who can donate?

Age: 18 to 66 years old Weight: 50 kg or more Health: In good health

NOTE: Requirements may vary slightly depending on local regulations

Frequency: Minimum 3 months gap between donations.

Who should not donate?

Recent illness or infection Chronic diseases Recent surgery Pregnant or breastfeeding

NOTE: Requirements may vary slightly depending on local regulations

If unsure, consult with medical staff before donating.

Donation Process
Registration and health screening Mini physical examination Blood donation (10-15 minutes) Rest and refreshments Resume normal activities

Benefits of Donating Blood

Save up to 3 lives with one donation Free health check-up Reduce risk of heart disease Burn calories Boost your mental well-being

Feel good knowing you're making a difference!

Donation Tips

Stay Hydrated

Drink plenty of water before and after donation.

Eat Well

Have a healthy meal before donating blood.

Get Rest

Sleep well the night before your donation.

Avoid Smoking

Do not smoke at least 1 hour before and after donation.

Relax

Stay calm and breathe normally during the process.

Post Donation Care

Avoid heavy exercise and liftings for 24 hours.
