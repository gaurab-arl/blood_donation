How it works

A simple, safe and streamlined process designed to make your contribution as impactful as possible.

Registration

Create a profile in minutes. We'll ask basic questions to ensure you are eligible to donate safely.

Health Screening

A quick mini-physical at the center. We check your temperature, blood pressure, and hemoglobin level.

Donation

Sit back and relax. The actual donation takes about 8–10 minutes for a pint of whole blood.

Refresh & Recovery

Enjoy a snack and a drink for 15 minutes before resuming your day, knowing you've saved lives.
